# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants must communicate respectfully, accept constructive feedback,
focus on the technical issue, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats,
unwanted sexual attention, or publication of another person's private
information.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately at
[2335980262@qq.com](mailto:2335980262@qq.com). Reports will be reviewed
confidentially as availability permits and addressed in proportion to their
impact, including warnings, temporary participation limits, or permanent
exclusion.

This policy applies in project spaces and when a participant publicly
represents the project.

## Attribution

This concise policy follows the responsibilities and enforcement principles of
the Contributor Covenant 2.1 without reproducing its full text.
